# ShopSphere Interview Questions

# Day 1 - Project Introduction

## Q1. Tell me about your project.

Answer:

ShopSphere is a production-ready Full Stack E-Commerce platform developed using the MERN Stack. It allows users to register, browse products, search, filter, add items to the cart, place orders, track deliveries, review products, and manage their profiles. The application also includes an Admin Dashboard for product, order, and user management. The project follows real software engineering practices from requirement gathering to deployment.

---

## Q2. Why did you choose an E-Commerce project?

Answer:

An E-Commerce platform combines frontend, backend, authentication, database management, REST APIs, payment integration, deployment, and security into one application. It closely resembles real-world software products and demonstrates full-stack development skills.

---

## Q3. What problem does your application solve?

Answer:

The application provides customers with a secure and convenient way to purchase products online while helping businesses manage products, customers, inventory, and orders efficiently.

---

## Q4. Who are the users of your application?

Answer:

The application has two main users.

1. Customers
2. Administrators

Customers purchase products, while administrators manage the entire platform.

## Q6. Why did you choose MongoDB instead of MySQL?

Answer:

MongoDB is a NoSQL database that stores data in flexible JSON-like documents. It works naturally with JavaScript, scales easily, and is well-suited for applications like e-commerce where product structures can vary.

---

## Q7. Why are you following documentation before coding?

Answer:

Professional software companies first gather requirements, prepare architecture, design the database, define APIs, and then start implementation. This reduces confusion, improves planning, and minimizes rework.

---

## Q8. What development methodology are you following?

Answer:

We are following an SDLC-inspired approach with iterative development. Each feature is planned, documented, implemented, tested, committed to Git, and then reviewed before moving to the next module.

---

## Q9. What is the future scope of ShopSphere?

Answer:

Future enhancements include AI-powered recommendations, voice search, Progressive Web App support, real-time order tracking, multi-language support, analytics dashboards, and microservices architecture.
