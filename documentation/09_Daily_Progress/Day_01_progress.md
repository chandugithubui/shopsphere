Customers need an online shopping platform where they can easily:

- Register an account
- Browse products
- Search products
- Filter and sort products
- Add products to the cart
- Purchase products securely
- Track their orders
- Review purchased products

# Requirement Analysis Questions

Today, I answered the following project planning questions before starting development:

### 1. What problem are we solving?

We are building an online marketplace that allows customers to purchase products easily from anywhere through a secure and user-friendly shopping experience.

### 2. Who are the target users?

Customers who want to browse, search, compare, and purchase products online, along with administrators who manage products, orders, users, and inventory.

### 3. What are the core features?

- User Registration
- User Login
- Product Listing
- Product Details
- Search Products
- Filter & Sort Products
- Shopping Cart
- Wishlist
- Checkout
- Payment
- Order Tracking
- Product Reviews
- Admin Dashboard

### 4. Why are we choosing the MERN Stack?

The MERN Stack provides a complete JavaScript-based ecosystem for building scalable, modern, and responsive full-stack web applications with faster development and easier maintenance.

### 5. What is the overall project goal?

To build a production-ready, scalable, secure, and responsive E-Commerce platform following real software engineering practices and industry standards.
